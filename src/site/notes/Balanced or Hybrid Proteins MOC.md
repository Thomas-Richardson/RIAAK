aka hybrid proteins aka blended proteins

Basically adding 20-30% veg or mushrooms or plant based meat into regular meat.


# Advantages
- Adds fibre
- Taste tests show same or better taste
- Sometimes burgers have less shrinkage, holds onto juices
- Cost advantages (see below)
- [Polling by Food Systems Innovations](https://www.greenqueen.com.hk/blended-hybrid-meat-kids-meals-fast-food-healthy-protein/) suggests that they are quite popular in kids' meals.
- Some products are cost competitive with animal products
